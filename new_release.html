New Updates Available in v0.1.4

  • Added new game mode: Shooting range – a single player time attack & scored challenge. [Check a demo!]
  • Battle result screen now includes game session scores.
  • Loading time reduced in half!
  • Improved controls:
    • Swipe length & speed determines shot’s power.
    • Cancel flick by swiping down before releasing.
    • Improved double-tap functionality – removed accidental selections.
    • Improved pieces selection within piece view.
    • Ensure that the selected piece is not occluded by other pieces.
  • Improved Single Player's AI & rebalanced pieces masses.
  • Different type of shots can be achieved by starting the swipe in different parts of the piece:
    • Head – flick will be like Superman: head in the front.
    • Body – flick will be plain, piece will be stable in the board.
    • Foot – flick will have a slight angle, piece will elevate a bit.
  • Switch starting player on match restart in order to avoid first move advantage.
  • Blended and animated tutorial for newcomers.
  • Added a cool AI battle in the menu background.
